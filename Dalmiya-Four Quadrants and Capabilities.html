Capability Hierarchy (Manis & Madhavaram, 2023)

Internal Productivity

(Automation of internal tasks)

Sales coach as a personalized assistant. (CC)

Talking points and dealer specific. Saves time as a personalised MIS.

Integrated capability : Sales force management capability

Customer response capability

Internal Growth

(Informs decision which affects revenue)

Churn Model (BC)

Predictive AI uses internal data to identify dealer attrition

Composite capability : Market Orientation

Churn Model + Sales Workflow (CC)

Decision making for frontline usability -sales officers can see this integrated into their workflow and can make appropriate decisions.

Integrated capability : Sales force management capability

Pricing Algorithm (CC)

Pricing based on local market conditions and geography

Composite capability : Price-setting capability

External Productivity

(Improves operational capabilities with external stakeholders)

External Growth

Digital twin of the sales officer (BC)

engaging with smaller, long-tail dealers in human-like conversations to nurture relationships and even secure orders.

Integrated capability : Sales force management capability

Agentic AI with autonomous agents (CC)

Dynamic custom messages to increase premium products or handle outstanding payments.

Integrated capability : Market relating capability

Capability Levels

• BC – Basic Capability: Single analytics or isolated AI tool

• CC – Composite Capability: Multiple capabilities combined and integrated

• IC – Interconnected Capability: Deep, organization-wide, hard-to-imitate capability
